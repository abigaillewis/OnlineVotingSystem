I am currently completing a Computer Science Degree at the University of Warwick with a year abroad at Monash University. Over the course of my degree I have developed a large range of programming skills in languages such as Java, C and Haskell. Additionally, in terms of web development I have worked on two projects both of which used HTML, CSS, PHP, Javascript and a MySQL database. The first was a Todo list application that allowed users to create and add items to list. The second was a Bill Spliter, an application that allowed a group of University students to upload and divide household bills. This application also integrated email notifications that informed users when new bills were added.
